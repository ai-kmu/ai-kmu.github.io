Research Topics


Deep Learning

We are interested in innovative deep learning algorithms and applications for large-scale data analysis and data-driven learning that can deliver breakthrough performance.

Artificial Intelligence

We are creating scalable and accurate artificial intelligence algorithms based on recent breakthroughs in deep learning, machine learning, data mining, and statistical/adaptive signal processing.

Systems for Intelligence

We are developing technology solutions that can implement and accelerate our analytics engines with emphasis on intelligent memory/storage systems and massively parallel/distributed architectures.

People


Research Professor




Jaekoo Lee, PhD

Professor of Computer Engineering
jaekoo@kookmin.ac.kr
02-910-4646


Jaekoo Lee received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University, South Korea, in 2018.
From 2011 to 2013, he was with LG Electronics R\&D Campus, Seoul, Korea. He held Data Scientist at SK Telecom Corporation, in 2018.
He is currently an Assistant Professor with College of Computer Science, Kookmin University, South Korea.
His research interests include artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, deep learning, data science, and their applications.
